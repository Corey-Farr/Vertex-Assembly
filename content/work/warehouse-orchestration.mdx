## Overview

A comprehensive warehouse orchestration system managing the seamless coordination of 150+ autonomous guided vehicles, robotic picking stations, and automated storage/retrieval systems across a 500,000 square foot fulfillment center.

<Figure caption="Aerial view of the orchestrated warehouse floor" aspectRatio="16/9" />

## The Challenge

Modern e-commerce fulfillment demands unprecedented operational complexity:

- **Order volume** exceeding 100,000 orders per day
- **SKU diversity** with 500,000+ unique products
- **Delivery expectations** requiring same-day shipping cutoffs
- **Seasonal peaks** with 400% volume spikes
- **Labor constraints** in a tight employment market

<Callout type="info" title="Complexity Scale">
At peak operation, the system coordinates over 2,000 simultaneous pick/pack/ship workflows, making real-time decisions every 50 milliseconds.
</Callout>

## Our Solution

We developed an intelligent orchestration platform that treats the entire warehouse as a unified system:

### Fleet Management Intelligence

The AGV fleet operates as a coordinated organism:

- **Dynamic path planning** avoiding congestion before it occurs
- **Predictive positioning** placing vehicles where they'll be needed
- **Battery optimization** scheduling charging during natural lulls
- **Traffic flow management** creating express lanes during peaks

<Figure caption="AGV fleet navigating optimized traffic patterns" aspectRatio="4/3" />

### Robotic Picking Integration

Picking stations achieve remarkable throughput:

- **Goods-to-person** workflow eliminating picker travel
- **AI-powered item recognition** handling unknown SKUs
- **Ergonomic presentation** reducing fatigue and errors
- **Multi-order batching** optimizing pick sequences

### Automated Storage Systems

High-density storage maximizes space:

- **Shuttle-based AS/RS** with 150,000 bin positions
- **Cube storage technology** for small items
- **Automated pallet handling** for bulk storage
- **Dynamic slotting** continuously optimizing locations

<Callout type="success" title="Space Efficiency">
The automated storage systems achieve 3x the storage density of traditional shelving while reducing retrieval time by 70%.
</Callout>

### Real-Time Orchestration Engine

The brain of the operation:

- **Order orchestration** balancing priorities and SLAs
- **Resource allocation** matching tasks to capabilities
- **Exception handling** automatically resolving issues
- **Continuous optimization** learning from every operation
